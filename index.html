    Wands for Wildlife®

    I am excited to host a Wandraiser for Wands for Wildlife®! I will be collecting used mascara wands and sending them to the Appalachian Wildlife Refuge. Old mascara wands are used to remove fly eggs and larva from the fur of wild animals. They work great because the bristles are close together. Recycle and save wild lives!
